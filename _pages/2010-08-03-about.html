What up!? Welcome to my blog, mosts posts will revolve around my personal interests, that is: technology (linux and unix, hardware, networking, high performance computing, computer vision, upcoming trends and innovation, etc), modern arts (photography, and movies), music, science and mathematics.

Anyway, I don't feel I am a definitive source of information, I just want to share problems I encounter, my solution to some of these problems, some of my code, my opinion with regard to certain issues, etc. Hopefully, if I struggle with something and eventually get around to solving it, that can save others a whole lot of time and trouble. You know how it is with blogs, sometimes I will simply talk about my particular opinion or perspective with respect to something.... sometimes I might get a little personal, or emotional... you may agree, you may not, but you know it's all good we can all still get along!!! :-)

I am a Computer Engineer out of Universidad Autononoma de Madrid (UAM), and currently enrolled in their MSc. program in Computer and Electrical Engineering and working as a research assistant at the High Performance Computing and Networking research group. My particular research interests regard OS and driver optimization (linux), NUMA architectures, network protocols and hardware.

Anyway, love you all and peace!
